Information

Another vertical tab menu, this time for 'Khubsurat Graphics', who asked if this type of menu would be possible.

This one makes use of 'transparency' for the flyout sublevel which will not validate. You can omit the transparency if you require CSS validation.

The flyouts in this demonstration can hold from one to fifteen links, but with a change of flyout dimensions they can hold as many as you like.

This has been tested in IE5.5, IE6, IE7, Firefox and Opera.
